DESCRIPTION

Mask Heist is a puzzle-stealth game set during a single night inside a grand museum. Players explore themed exhibition rooms and carefully plan their perfect heist while avoiding detection and solving environmental challenges.

The core mechanic revolves around magical masks that unlock new abilities and ways to interact with the world. Each mask transforms how the player moves, hides, or manipulates the environment, encouraging experimentation and strategic thinking. By switching masks, players can adapt their playstyle and discover creative solutions to obstacles.

My role in the project was multidisciplinary. I worked on gameplay programming, implementing core mechanics, mask abilities, and stealth systems. I was also responsible for 3D art production, creating assets and contributing to the visual identity of the environments.

Additionally, I composed and implemented the game’s music, designing the soundtrack to reinforce tension, atmosphere, and player immersion throughout the heist experience.
