Bubble Gum Royale

ROLE:
Programmer & Designer
GAME JAM:
Global Game Jam 2025
PLATFORM:
PC (Itch.io)
GAME ENGINE:
Unity Engine

DESCRIPTION

Bubble Gum Royale is a fun and fast-paced competitive game where players take control of a living piece of bubble gum battling inside a chaotic arena. Only one player can survive — and staying inside the arena is everything.

Players must avoid falling off the stage while pushing opponents out, racing to secure victory before their friends steal all the chocolate. The game focuses on quick reactions, dynamic movement, and unpredictable multiplayer interactions, creating intense and humorous matches.

Developed during the UCM Global Game Jam 2025, the project was created under tight time constraints, emphasizing rapid prototyping, strong core mechanics, and accessible competitive gameplay.

My role in the project involved both Programming and Game Design. I implemented the core gameplay systems, player movement mechanics, and multiplayer interactions, while also contributing to the overall design direction, balancing the arena dynamics and refining the competitive experience to ensure chaotic yet fair gameplay.
