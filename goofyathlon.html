DESCRIPTION

This game is built around a collection of dynamic minigames focused on movement and reaction time, where players compete to achieve the highest possible score. Each challenge is designed to test coordination, timing, and precision through fast-paced and engaging gameplay.

The minigames are intuitive and easy to learn, making the experience accessible to all types of players while still offering room for skill improvement and mastery. The design emphasizes clarity, responsiveness, and immediate feedback to maintain a fun and competitive atmosphere.

A key feature of the project is its innovative control system. Players can take control of their character using body movement, as the main control scheme was developed to use a mobile phone as a motion-based controller (instructions are included in the README file). The game can also be played using a traditional keyboard setup.

My role in the project was primarily Programmer and Designer. I was responsible for implementing the core gameplay systems, developing the movement control logic, integrating the mobile controller functionality, and designing the structure and mechanics of the minigames to ensure both accessibility and engaging gameplay flow.
