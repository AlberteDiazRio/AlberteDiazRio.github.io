DESCRIPTION

This puzzle-adventure game places you in the role of a wizard trapped inside a mysterious dungeon. Armed with only a single spell and your own determination, you must explore dark chambers and uncover a way to escape — but nothing inside the dungeon behaves as expected.

The core mechanic revolves around manipulating the dungeon itself, shifting rooms and structures as if solving a sliding puzzle. Inspired by the classic Super Slide game, the experience challenges players to think spatially and strategically, combining environmental movement with exploration-based progression.

As the dungeon rearranges and hidden pathways emerge, players must carefully observe patterns and anticipate changes in order to solve increasingly complex puzzles and reach freedom.

My role in the project involved both Programming and Game Design. I was responsible for implementing the core puzzle mechanics, dungeon movement systems, and interaction logic. I also contributed to the overall game design, defining the structure of the puzzles, balancing difficulty progression, and ensuring a coherent gameplay flow from start to finish.
