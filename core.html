DESCRIPTION

Project Core is a dynamic and competitive online multiplayer turn-based card game where two players face off in strategic battles. Players summon robotic units and evolve them through distinct evolutionary branches, adapting their strategy as the match unfolds.

Each robot features unique abilities and progression paths, encouraging tactical decision-making and long-term planning. The game combines deck-building elements with real-time competitive tension, rewarding players who can anticipate their opponent’s moves and optimize their evolution strategy.

My role in the project involved both Programming and Game Design. I was responsible for implementing the core turn-based systems, online multiplayer functionality, and evolution mechanics. Additionally, I contributed to the overall game design, balancing robot abilities, defining progression branches, and refining the competitive flow to ensure engaging and fair matches.
